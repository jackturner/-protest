“You’ll Never Know We Were Here”

by Fernando Orellana

The robots in the intallation, You’ll Never Know We Were Here, are designed to create an anonymous protest, continuously moving protest signs up and down. Unlike their human counterparts, these robot activists do not have identities, they do not worry about reprisal for broadcasting their messages. The voice you leave on this website may become part of the protest during the exhibition of this artwork at the Spring Break Art Show in New York City.
